Our Story

From humble beginnings to your favorite local coffee spot in Genshinville

Who We Are

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts Furina and Aether, Bean There Café began as a small kiosk with a big dream: to bring exceptional coffee to our community.

What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved neighborhood gathering place, but our commitment to quality and community has never wavered.

We meticulously source our beans from sustainable farms, roast them in small batches to bring out their unique characteristics, and prepare each drink with care and precision.
